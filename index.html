About Jeenadine

Jeenadine M. Guavis, LPT, MAEd, is a passionate educator and research leader in the Lasallian community. She has served as HUMSS Strand Head, Research and Immersion Coordinator, Managing Editor, and faculty trainer with over a decade of service in shaping young minds and nurturing Christian values.
